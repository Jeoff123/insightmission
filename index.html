About

Insight Mission is a Human Resource Development initiative committed to fostering deep education, meaningful careers, and an enriched quality of life among young people. Founded in 1998 in Mavelikara, Kerala, by Sunil D. Kuruvilla—a distinguished human development trainer, educator, career counsellor, and passionate speaker—Insight Mission has grown into a dynamic organisation with a wide reach across Kerala and beyond.

Our Mission

Our core mission is the holistic development of young people. We believe in nurturing their potential, guiding them towards the right career paths, and supporting them in leading purposeful lives. To achieve this, we engage in continuous research, design innovative solutions to youth-related challenges, and partner with various institutions to deliver high-impact programs.
